The stockings are hung. The tree is lit. And your toddler? They are bouncing off the walls.

It's December 23rd in **San Jose**, and the anticipation is palpable. While we adults might feel stressed, toddlers feel pure, unadulterated *energy*. They know something big is coming, and their little bodies just can't contain it.

But a hyperactive toddler usually leads to a sleepless night, which leads to a cranky Christmas morning. Nobody wants that.

Here are three ways to help your little one wind down when the holiday jitters hit.

### 1. Burn the Energy Early (Heavy Work)
Don't keep them cooped up inside looking at the presents. Their nervous system needs to discharge that excitement.
Take them to a playground in **Campbell** or **Sunnyvale** and encourage "Heavy Work."
*   Climbing up the slide.
*   Pushing a heavy wagon.
*   Carrying grocery bags (the light ones!).
Heavy work provides "proprioceptive input," which is incredibly grounding and calming for the brain.

### 2. The "Warm Down" Routine
Tonight is not the night to skip the bath. In fact, make it longer.
Warm water is a natural muscle relaxant. Add a drop of lavender oil if you have it.
After the bath, try a little "pizza massage." Pretend their back is the dough, knead it (gently), add the sauce (rubbing), and the cheese (light tickles). Physical touch releases oxytocin, the "cuddle hormone," which combats cortisol (the stress hormone).

### 3. Walk Through the Plan
Sometimes, excitement is actually anxiety in disguise. "What is happening? Is Santa scary? Will I get the toy?"
Remove the mystery. Sit down and explain exactly what tomorrow will look like.
*"First, we will wake up. Then we will eat pancakes. Then we will open presents."*
Knowing the schedule gives them a sense of safety, allowing their brain to relax enough to sleep.

### A Silent Night (Hopefully)
If they still wake up at 5 AM? Well, that's just part of the magic. Grab your coffee, take a deep breath, and enjoy the wonder in their eyes.

From all of us at Sunny Child Care, we wish you a peaceful and restful night before the festivities begin.

襪子掛好了。聖誕樹點亮了。而你的幼兒呢？他們正在家裡飛簷走壁。

今天是 **San Jose** 的 12 月 23 日，空氣中充滿了期待。雖然我們大人可能感到壓力，但幼兒感受到的是純粹、未經過濾的*能量*。他們知道有大事要發生了，他們小小的身體簡直無法容納這股興奮。

但是，過度亢奮的幼兒通常意味著失眠的夜晚，這又會導致聖誕節早晨的起床氣。沒人想要那樣。

當節日躁動來襲時，這裡有三個方法可以幫助你的小寶貝平靜下來。

### 1. 提早消耗能量（本體覺活動）
不要讓他們整天關在家裡盯著禮物看。他們的神經系統需要釋放那股興奮感。
帶他們去 **Campbell** 或 **Sunnyvale** 的公園，鼓勵他們做一些「負重活動 (Heavy Work)」。
*   爬滑梯。
*   推重重的推車。
*   幫忙提購物袋（輕的那種！）。
負重活動能提供「本體覺輸入」，這對大腦來說有極佳的穩定和鎮靜效果。

### 2. 「降溫」儀式
今晚絕對不是省略洗澡的時候。事實上，洗久一點。
溫水是天然的肌肉鬆弛劑。如果你有的話，加一滴薰衣草精油。
洗完澡後，試試看「披薩按摩」。假裝他們的背是麵團，揉一揉（輕輕地），加醬汁（搓揉），撒起司（輕輕搔癢）。肢體接觸會釋放催產素（擁抱荷爾蒙），這能對抗皮質醇（壓力荷爾蒙）。

### 3. 預告明天的行程
有時候，興奮其實是偽裝的焦慮。「會發生什麼事？聖誕老公公可怕嗎？我會拿到玩具嗎？」
消除神秘感。坐下來，清楚地解釋明天會發生什麼事。
*「首先，我們會起床。然後我們會吃鬆餅。接著我們會拆禮物。」*
知道行程表能給他們安全感，讓大腦放鬆下來，足以入睡。

### 平安夜（希望是安靜的）
如果他們明天早上 5 點就醒來？好吧，這也是魔法的一部分。拿著你的咖啡，深呼吸，享受他們眼中的驚奇吧。

Sunny Child Care 的全體同仁，祝你在慶祝活動開始前，有一個平靜且充分休息的夜晚。
