It is December 31st. The last sunset of 2025 over the **Santa Cruz Mountains**.

As we prepare to ring in the New Year, it is natural to look back. We scroll through our phones, looking at photos from January, amazed at how much our children have physically grown. The pants that are too short, the baby fat that is melting away.

But at Sunny Child Care, we see a different kind of growth. We see the "invisible" milestones. These aren't the ones you mark on a wall with a pencil, but they are the ones that shape who your child is becoming.

Here are three "Invisible Victories" I want you to celebrate tonight.

### 1. The Birth of Empathy
Remember that time in March when your toddler snatched a toy and hit their friend?
Now, look at them in December. Maybe they brought you a tissue when you sneezed. Maybe they patted a crying baby on the back.
That shift—from "Me" to "We"—is monumental. It is the foundation of kindness. If you saw even a glimmer of empathy this year, celebrate it.

### 2. The "I Can Do It" Spirit
In January, they might have waited for you to put on their shoes.
Now, they might be insisting on zipping their own jacket (even if it takes 10 minutes and they miss the track).
This struggle for independence can be frustrating for parents, but it is a huge victory for the child. It means they believe in their own capability. They are building the confidence to tackle the world.

### 3. The Explosion of "Why?"
I know, the endless questions can be exhausting. *"Why is the sky blue?" "Why does the dog bark?"*
But think about what this means. Their brains are waking up to the complexity of the world. They are no longer just observing; they are analyzing. They are little scientists and philosophers.
Cherish the "Why." It means their curiosity is alive and well.

### A Toast to You, Parents
And finally, a milestone for *you*.
You survived the tantrums. You navigated the sleepless nights. You balanced work and family in the busy **Bay Area**.
You are a stronger, wiser, and more patient parent than you were 365 days ago.

Raise a glass (of champagne or sparkling cider) tonight. You did it.

Happy New Year from all of us at Sunny Child Care! We can't wait to see what 2026 brings.

今天是 12 月 31 日。2025 年的最後一道夕陽正落在 **Santa Cruz Mountains** 上。

當我們準備迎接新年時，回顧過去是很自然的。我們滑著手機，看著一月份的照片，驚訝於孩子在身體上長大了多少。那些變短的褲子，那些逐漸消失的嬰兒肥。

但在 Sunny Child Care，我們看見的是另一種成長。我們看見那些「看不見」的里程碑。這些不是你能用鉛筆在牆上做記號的高度，但它們塑造了你的孩子正在成為什麼樣的人。

今晚，我希望你們慶祝這三個「看不見的勝利」。

### 1. 同理心的萌芽
還記得三月的時候，你的孩子搶了玩具還打了朋友嗎？
現在，看看十二月的他們。也許當你打噴嚏時，他們會遞給你一張衛生紙。也許他們會輕拍正在哭泣的寶寶的背。
這種從「我」到「我們」的轉變是巨大的。這是善良的基石。如果你今年看到了哪怕是一絲絲同理心的光芒，請慶祝它。

### 2. 「我可以自己做」的精神
在一月，他們可能還等著你幫忙穿鞋。
現在，他們可能堅持要自己拉外套拉鍊（即使要花 10 分鐘而且還拉錯軌道）。
這種對獨立的爭取對父母來說可能很挫折，但對孩子來說是一個巨大的勝利。這代表他們相信自己的能力。他們正在建立面對世界的自信。

### 3. 「為什麼」的大爆發
我知道，無止盡的問題讓人很累。*「為什麼天空是藍色的？」「為什麼狗狗會叫？」*
但想想這意味著什麼。他們的大腦正在甦醒，開始理解世界的複雜性。他們不再只是觀察；他們在分析。他們是小小的科學家和哲學家。
珍惜這些「為什麼」。這代表他們的好奇心正蓬勃發展。

### 向你們致敬，爸媽們
最後，這是一個屬於*你們*的里程碑。
你們撐過了每一次崩潰。你們度過了無數個失眠的夜晚。你們在忙碌的 **Bay Area** 平衡了工作與家庭。
比起 365 天前，你們成為了更堅強、更有智慧、更有耐心的父母。

今晚，舉起酒杯（香檳或氣泡蘋果汁都好）。你們做到了。

Sunny Child Care 全體同仁祝大家新年快樂！我們迫不及待想看看 2026 年會帶來什麼驚喜。
