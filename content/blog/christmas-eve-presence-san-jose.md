It’s finally here. Christmas Eve.

If you are like most parents in **San Jose**, the last few weeks have been a blur of shopping, wrapping, baking, and coordinating schedules. You might be exhausted. You might be wondering if you did "enough."

*Did I get the right toy? Did I move the Elf on the Shelf last night? Is the turkey thawed?*

I want you to take a deep breath. Stop. Look at your child.

At Sunny Child Care, we see children every day. And I can tell you a secret: They don't care about the perfect bow on the gift. They don't care if the potatoes are slightly lumpy.

What they want—what they *really* crave—is you.

### The Magic of "Now"
Tonight, I invite you to give your child the most expensive gift in the world: Your undivided attention.

**Put down the phone.**
I know, you want to capture every cute moment for Instagram. But try to capture a few moments just for your own heart. Look at them with your eyes, not through a screen.

**Let them play with the box.**
We laugh about it, but it’s true. Toddlers love the wrapping paper and the boxes as much as the gift. Don't rush them to "open the next one." Let them explore. Let them be little.

**Embrace the mess.**
There will be paper on the floor. There will be crumbs. It’s okay. The mess means memories are being made.

### A Wish for You
To all our wonderful families in the Bay Area, thank you for trusting us with your most precious treasures. Watching your children grow is the greatest gift we could ask for.

May your Christmas Eve be filled with less "perfect" and more "present." May your home be warm, your heart be light, and your coffee be strong tomorrow morning!

Merry Christmas!

終於到了。平安夜。

如果你像大多數 **San Jose** 的家長一樣，過去這幾週簡直是一場由購物、包裝、烘焙和協調行程組成的混戰。你可能已經筋疲力盡。你可能在懷疑自己做得「夠不夠」。

*我買對玩具了嗎？我昨晚有記得移動那個聖誕小精靈 (Elf on the Shelf) 嗎？火雞解凍了嗎？*

我希望你深呼吸。停下來。看看你的孩子。

在 Sunny Child Care，我們每天都看著孩子。我可以告訴你一個秘密：他們不在乎禮物上的蝴蝶結有沒有打好。他們不在乎馬鈴薯泥是不是有點結塊。

他們想要的——他們*真正*渴望的——是你。

### 「當下」的魔法
今晚，我邀請你送給孩子世界上最昂貴的禮物：你全心全意的關注。

**放下手機。**
我知道，你想為了 Instagram 捕捉每一個可愛的瞬間。但試著為你自己的心捕捉幾個瞬間。用你的眼睛看著他們，而不是透過螢幕。

**讓他們玩盒子。**
我們常拿這件事開玩笑，但這是真的。幼兒對包裝紙和紙盒的喜愛程度，不亞於禮物本身。不要催促他們「快點開下一個」。讓他們去探索。讓他們當個孩子。

**擁抱混亂。**
地板上會有包裝紙。會有餅乾屑。沒關係的。混亂代表著回憶正在被創造。

### 給你的祝福
致我們在灣區所有美好的家庭，謝謝你們將最珍貴的寶貝託付給我們。看著你們的孩子成長，是我們所能收到最好的禮物。

願你的平安夜少一點「完美」，多一點「陪伴」。願你的家溫暖，你的心輕盈，願你明天早上的咖啡夠濃！

聖誕快樂！
