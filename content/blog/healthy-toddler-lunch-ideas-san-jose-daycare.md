## The "No" Phase

If I had a dollar for every time a parent sat in my office in **San Jose**, sighed, and said, *"He just won't eat anything green,"* I’d be a rich woman. I get it. One day they love bananas, the next day bananas are the enemy. It’s exhausting.

But here at **Sunny Child Care**, we serve breakfast, lunch, and dinner to hundreds of children, and guess what? We get them to eat. (Yes, even the broccoli).

It’s not magic. It’s about creating the right environment. We believe that food is love, and when children feel safe and happy, they eat better. Here are three secrets from our kitchen that you can try at home.

### 1. The Power of "Warm & Comforting" (The Soup Trick)

In many preschools, lunch is a cold sandwich. But at Sunny, we believe in the power of a **warm meal**. There is something deeply comforting about hot food, especially for little ones.

> **Try this:** Instead of dry snacks, try offering a small cup of warm soup. At our center, our **hearty soups** are a favorite. The warmth calms them down, and it’s a great way to blend in veggies they might otherwise refuse.

### 2. Family Style: "I Want What She's Having"

Have you ever noticed your child eats better when their friend is eating? We serve our meals **family-style**. Children sit together, pass the bowls (with help!), and serve themselves.

> **Try this:** Eat *with* your child. Don't just feed them while you check your phone. Sit down, pour yourself a glass of **organic milk** (just like we serve!), and say, *"Mmm, this is delicious."* Positive peer pressure works wonders, even if the "peer" is Mom or Dad.

### 3. Celebrate the Seasons

Kids get bored with the same old apples and carrots. We keep things exciting by rotating **seasonal fruits**. When a child sees a bright red strawberry in summer or a crisp pear in autumn, they are curious.

> **Try this:** Take your child to a local **San Jose** farmers market. Let them pick out one "new" fruit to try. When they have ownership over the choice, they are 100% more likely to eat it.

## 「不要、不要」的挑食期

如果每當有家長坐在我位於**聖荷西 (San Jose)** 的辦公室裡，嘆著氣說：「他就是不吃綠色的蔬菜」，我就能得到一塊錢的話，我現在應該是大富翁了。我完全懂。昨天他們還超愛香蕉，今天香蕉就變成了頭號敵人。這真的很讓人心累。

但在 **Sunny Child Care**，我們每天供應早餐、午餐和晚餐給數百名孩子，你猜怎麼著？我們有辦法讓他們乖乖吃飯。（沒錯，甚至是花椰菜）。

這不是魔法。這是關於創造正確的環境。我們相信「食物就是愛」，當孩子感到安全和快樂時，他們會吃得更好。以下是我們廚房的三個小秘密，你也可以在家試試看。

### 1. 「暖心熱食」的力量 (湯品小撇步)

在許多幼兒園，午餐可能只是冰冷的三明治。但在 Sunny，我們堅信**熱騰騰正餐**的力量。熱食有一種深層的安撫作用，特別是對小小孩來說。

> **試試這個小撇步：** 與其給乾乾的零食，不如試著給一小杯熱湯。在我們中心，我們的**營養湯品**可是人氣王。溫暖的感覺能讓孩子平靜下來，而且這是一個把他們平常抗拒的蔬菜「藏」進去的好方法。

### 2. 家庭式用餐：「我也要吃那個！」

你有沒有發現，當朋友在吃東西時，你的孩子也會吃得比較好？我們採用**家庭式 (Family-style)** 的用餐方式。孩子們坐在一起，傳遞碗盤（當然有老師幫忙！），然後自己盛飯。

> **試試這個小撇步：** 和你的孩子*一起*吃飯。不要只是餵他們，然後自己在一旁滑手機。坐下來，給自己倒一杯**有機牛奶**（就像我們學校供應的一樣！），然後說：「嗯～這好好吃喔。」正向的同儕壓力非常有效，即使這個「同儕」是爸爸或媽媽。

### 3. 跟著季節吃

如果老是吃一樣的蘋果和紅蘿蔔，孩子也是會膩的。我們會輪替供應**當季水果**來保持新鮮感。當孩子看到夏天鮮紅的草莓或秋天清脆的梨子時，他們會感到好奇。

> **試試這個小撇步：** 帶你的孩子去**聖荷西**當地的農夫市集。讓他們自己挑選一種「新」水果來嘗試。當他們對選擇擁有主導權時，他們願意嘗試的機率絕對會提高 100%。
